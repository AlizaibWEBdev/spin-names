Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Wheel of Names

Is the Wheel of Names really free to use?

Yes! Our Wheel of Names is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can spin the wheel as many times as you want without any limitations.

How many items can I add to the wheel?

You can add up to 100 items to the wheel. Each item should be on its own line in the input box.

Can I save my wheel configuration?

Yes, you can save your wheel configuration by clicking the "Save Wheel" button. This will generate a unique link that you can bookmark to return to your exact setup.

Is the wheel spinner mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Our Wheel of Names works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adjusts automatically to your screen size.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes, you're welcome to use our Wheel of Names for commercial purposes like business giveaways or team activities. We only ask that you don't rebrand or resell the tool itself.
